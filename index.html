Some Stuff About Me

WHO I AM - First & foremost, I am a Dad. I have 2 boys, 1 year old Bryce & 2 year old Bronson. I grew up in a military family, moving frequently to Army bases all over the east coast. After settling down in the Richmond area of Virginia, I consider that my hometown. I have lived in Orlando, FL for about a decade now & will soon be saying "Good-bye!" to the sweltering, unrelenting heat. My wife & I have decided to relocate to Massachusetts, where her family lives. We will be living in the Greater Boston Area, trying to stay within 45 minutes of the city. Our goal is to be there by March 2016. I am Co-Owner, along with my Mother, of a successful neighborhood bar & grill called The Pour House.

I enjoyed the business before I had kids. Late nights, long hours & the bar scene is not the environment I want my boys to grow up in. I planned on changing careers about a year ago & was introduced to the world of Web Development by my Sister & 2 Cousins (who are all back-end developers) as a career that I'd be perfect for. They were right. I enrolled in a Rails Engineering bootcamp at The Iron Yard in May 2015. The 12 week course was intense, immersive & game changing for me. Some other important stuff about me: Star Wars over Star Trek, Biggie Over Tupac, I love to laugh & love to make my friends laugh even more, I'm not great at writing about myself & most importantly, I am ready & eager to start my new career as a Web Developer.
